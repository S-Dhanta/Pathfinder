Marketing Manager

Marketing Managers develop and execute strategies to promote products, services, and brands effectively.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and implement marketing campaigns.
  • Analyze market trends and competitor strategies.
  • Manage social media, branding, and advertising efforts.
  • Collaborate with sales and product teams.
  • Monitor and optimize campaign performance.

Required Skills

  • Strong communication and leadership skills.
  • Knowledge of digital marketing and SEO.
  • Ability to analyze consumer behavior and trends.
  • Experience with marketing tools (Google Analytics, Ads, CRM).
  • Creativity and problem-solving skills.
